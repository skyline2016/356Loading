frequently asked questions

How do I proceed through the check out process?

Once you have finished adding items to your Shopping Cart, simply click the “Cart” button on the upper right corner in every page and we will guide you through the check out process.

Can you ship your products anywhere?

Yes! However, we are not responsible for additional charges you may incur as a result of international duties.

What methods of payment do you accept?

We accept all major Hong Kong credit card brands.

Do I need to install special software to shop online?

You must enable JavaScript in order to purchase items on our site. For specific instructions, please select your browser: Microsoft Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox, Opera, or Apple Safari. We also recommend that you install the latest version of Adobe Flash Player.

When will the order be delivery?

After we confirm your payment, your order will be sent out in 3 working days.

What is a fulfillment address?

The fulfillment address is your shipping address. If you have an international address, it is very helpful if you fill into your profile page.

How do I cancel an order or return an item?

E-mail us at cs@loading.net, letting us know that you will be returning an item. Then, ship the item with your receipt to 30, Good Shepherd Street, Ho Man Tin, Kowloon, Hong Kong . Please include in this shipment your personal information that you used to make the order. Our policy typically is that, for domestic returns, you as the customer will pay for shipping. This is to help streamline the process and avoid having to exchange money for the purpose of refunding items. For more details and info regarding in store returns, see our return policy.

Which browser should I use to view your site?

We highly recommend that you browse with the latest version of Microsoft Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox, or Apple Safari.

What can I do if my order is missing?

If you have not received your order within 14 working days of the date on which you ordered them then please contact us within 30 days of the date on which you ordered the products.

If you have other questions, please contact us.

Policy

Return & Refund

Returns

Refunds

You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.
Shipping
You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non­refundable.
If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.
